Music

I have been singing for as long as I can remember. I have been formally trained in Western Music and Hindustani Classical Music. I have written and composed many songs and even fully recorded one of them and gotten it produced. All that is left to do is release it! Stay tuned for my music coming out on your streaming platform soon! In the mean time, some of my favorite artists include: Mico, Hannah Bahng, Laufey, Tomorrow By Together, NCT Dream, Enhypen, Sabrina Carpenter, and Keshi. Go check them out!
